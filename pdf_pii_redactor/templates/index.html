About PDF PII Redactor

This tool automatically identifies and redacts personally identifiable information (PII) from PDF documents using OpenAI's API.

Supported PII types:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Addresses
  • Credit card numbers
  • Dates of birth

Note: Your PDF must contain selectable text. Scanned image PDFs are not supported.
